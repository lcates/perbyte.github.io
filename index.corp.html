About Us

Services

PerByte focuses on providing high quality software consulting to associations and non-profit organizations. Our goal is to help your organization optimize operations through our Association Management System (AMS) or Customer Relationship Management (CRM) implementation services, business process engineering, training, and custom software solutions. PerByte’s custom software solutions aim to improve process efficiencies and provide long-term value to your organization.

Our consulting services include:

  • Project management
  • Requirements analysis and documentation
  • User acceptance testing
  • Data conversion and data management
  • System configuration, including .NET software development, web services, and e-business
  • User training
  • Go-live support and stabilization
  • Future phase initiatives
  • Reporting and business intelligence
  • System upgrade
  • Business process engineering
